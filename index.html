Let's Relax

Introduction

Hi, User - Let's Relax

In our daily stressful life, we forget to breathe and actually relax. This app is aimed to heal that part.
The app plays mellifluent, soothing & relaxing music, to make you feel better.


What makes it different ?

It is a simple, minimalistic, and user-friendly music player, which is actually created to make you happy. This is what makes Mello, so different from others.


What's its Aim ?

Mello's aim is to make you happy, help you to feel relaxed, help you to be stress-free, help you to focus, help you to sleep.


It's the only app, that actually thinks about you.

Let's Start

Mello is a very user-friendly app.
There isn't any need of any kind of sign ups and sign ins, emails, passwords, otps, bla bla.

Just enter your Name, and you are ready to start the app.

Design

A Simple and Minimal Design to keep your eyes relaxed.

Music Category

There are 4 Music Categories -

Meditate
Work
Study
Sleep

Themes & Backgrounds

There are more than 30 backgrounds and a total of 3 themes for those backgrounds.

Storage

To save up space, music comes from the cloud storage, and the storage cache gets cleared automatically.

Dark mode

There's a feature of Automatic Dark Mode, which depends upon your timezone.

Sleep Timer

The app also comes with an useful feature - the SLEEP TIMER.
Here you can set a time of your wish, and the app will automatically turn off when the timer ends.
